Command :/nos
Rules:
- Player must have shot 55 barrels
- Player must be driving a vehicle
- Player must have $1000
- The vehicle must be NOS compatiable (e.g. no planes/helis/boats/bikes/trailers/trains/trams/quads)

- Validation error messages will show for the above in a clear, localised message with correct spelling/grammar.

- 10x nos will be added to the vehicle
- $1000 will be deducted from the player
- A notification will show that the NOS was successfully added
